A specialty coffee bar just outside Kotor's walls, on the waterfront square by the Kamelija centre. The focus is the cup: freshly roasted arabica pulled by skilled baristas, with flat whites and cappuccinos that regulars rate among the best in Montenegro, plus pastries and light bites. There's a terrace for people-watching and bay views; service can slow when a queue builds, so come a little off-peak if you're in a hurry. A good caffeine stop before or after the Old Town and the walls climb. Hours can shift seasonally — confirm on site.
