The **Made in New York Jazz Festival** returns to **Tivat** on **26 June 2026** for its **11th edition**, and it is **free to attend**. The open-air concert takes over the **Park Garden Stage at Porto Montenegro** from **21:00**, with the **John Scofield Trio**, the **Frank McComb Band** and special guest **Yaacov Mayman** on the bill. Pitched as part of the country's **20 years of independence** celebrations, it is one of the easiest big nights out on the coast this summer — no ticket required.

## What is on and when

The festival runs as a single evening on **26 June 2026**, starting at **21:00** on the **Park Garden Stage** inside the [Porto Montenegro](/cities/tivat/) marina village. Entry is **free and open to all** — there is no paid ticket, so arriving early to find a good spot is the smart move. An **after-party follows at SIRO Social from 23:00 to 01:00**, also open to everyone, with drinks for sale.

## Who is playing

The line-up leans into serious jazz. Guitarist **John Scofield** — a nine-time Grammy nominee who first broke through with Miles Davis — brings his trio, alongside soul-jazz vocalist and keyboardist **Frank McComb** and special guest saxophonist **Yaacov Mayman**. It is a bill that would carry a steep ticket price elsewhere, which is what makes the free format stand out.

## Why it is worth the trip

Porto Montenegro is the upscale heart of [Tivat](/cities/tivat/), an easy base on the Bay of Kotor, and a warm June evening by the marina is about as pleasant as a free concert gets. The date also slots neatly into an already busy coastal calendar — the [EXIT free festivals](/news/exit-festival-montenegro-2026/) bookend the summer further south, and [Budva's nightlife](/entertainment/budva-nightlife/) keeps going all season.

## Planning around it

Tivat sits a short drive from [Kotor](/cities/kotor/) and the rest of the bay, so it is easy to fold the concert into a wider coastal trip.
