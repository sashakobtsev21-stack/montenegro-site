An upscale seafood and modern-Mediterranean restaurant on the Šuranj waterfront, a few minutes' walk around the marina from the Old Town walls. Its signature is the open terrace looking straight across the Bay of Kotor to the town's ramparts and the mountains behind — one of the best dinner views in the bay. The kitchen works with fresh fish and a deep wine list, and it sits firmly at the top price level for Kotor. It's the place for a special meal rather than a quick bite, so book a terrace table in season and confirm the latest prices and hours on site.
