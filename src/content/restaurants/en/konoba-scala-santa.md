A traditional seafood konoba tucked onto Salad Square (Trg od Salate) inside Kotor's Old Town walls. It leans on the bay's fresh fish and shellfish — grilled squid and octopus, mussels, a well-known fish soup, black (squid-ink) risotto and the daily catch — served in a stone-walled room that's part of the Old Town's dining fabric. It sits at a mid price level for Kotor; the squares get crowded at peak dinner hours, so come early or late, and book ahead in high season. Prices and hours change — confirm the latest on site.
